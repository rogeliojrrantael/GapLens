GL GapLens
Semantic Discovery for PhD Researchers

Research Mapping Without Boolean Guesswork

Find research gaps with semantic search, publication trends, and country signals.

Explore who is publishing, how the topic has evolved over time, and where the literature still looks thin. This prototype uses a lightweight ML-style semantic engine instead of strict keyword AND/OR matching.

Try phrases like “gender bias in peer review”, “low-resource NLP for healthcare”, or “urban heat adaptation in Africa”.

What this helps with

  • Surfaces semantically related literature
  • Highlights countries actively publishing on the topic
  • Shows publication momentum across years
  • Points to underexplored methods, populations, and regions
Why semantic? Searching “doctoral wellbeing” can still match research framed as “graduate student mental health” or “researcher burnout”.
